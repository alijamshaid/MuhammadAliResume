    Dissertation: Estimating Vehicles Fuel Consumption and Emission Using COPERT Model in Apache Spark.

    My MS thesis focused on estimating vehicle fuel consumption and emissions using the COPERT model on high-resolution spatio-temporal GPS data. The data,was too large for conventional databases, therefore it was processed using Apache Spark. After cleaning and removing outliers, trajectories were classified into different driving scenarios. And other driving parameters such as velocity, acceleration, distance, and time were derived and used in the COPERT model implemented in Apache Spark using Python. Results showed that designed big data ecosystem has low latency of spatial queries from such massive data and results also showed that higher fuel consumption with less distance coverage in Karachi due to congestion, indicating significant energy inefficiency in peak hours while Rawalpindi and Islamabad has showed relatively similar results.
